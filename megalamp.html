    Induction Lamps.

    4th generation lighting solutions

    Introduction

    our retrofit solutions are designed to use your existing electrical fictures, there by saving valuable capital costs

    Electrodeless Magnetic induction lamps are basically flurecent lamps, but without filaments or electrodes hence the longe life. teo ferrite cores with coils are wrapped around the tube called an induction coil. high frequency ac from the electronic ballast is sent to the induction coil creating a very strong magnetic field which radiates through the glass tube and excites the amalgam (a solid compound of mercury and other elements), which is compacted in a solid glass pellet on the exterior of the lamp. the charged mercuty atoms emit UV rays that convert to visible light as it passes through the white phosphor coating in the tube.

    Advantages of Induction Lamp.

    • energy saving: >50% over other lamps MH, HPSV & HPMV
    • Long life: 60,000 - 10,000 hrs due to no filaments or electrodes
    • No stroboscopic effect no flicker and very low glare
    • Hot instant and repeat restart without cooling
    • Colour temp: available in warm white to cool whte 2700-6400k.
    • Colour: high colour rendering index > 80
    • High power factor: 0.98 and low THD < 5%.
    • Low lamp operating temperatue thus lower air conditioning costs
