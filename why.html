    Company Profile

    Established in the year 2008, we are team of professionals dedicatedly working in the field of Energy Saving. Mega Energy Solutions are one of the leading companies in India working in the Feild of Energy Saving/conservation products. We have Energy saving solutions for Street Lighting, Pumps and Motors, Central Air Conditioning, Window Split and Package Air Conditioners and we are manufacturing LED Lamps. We are supported by our efficient workforce. Our Energy Saving products are widely appreciated and demanded in different organizations for their quality and energy saving/conservation without affecting the end user. With our professional approach and association with reputed vendors, helps us to offer our clients with quality products at competitive prices, which complies to the International standards. We are backed by team of professionals working in the field of energy saving /conservation Products for more than 20 years.

    For Energy saving in Street Lighting, Pumps and Motors, Mega Energy Solutions have entered into an agreement with Enersine High Technology Ltd., Zhuhai, P R China. They have unique technology available with them for Street Lighting. We have been appointed by them as Distributor for the Territory of India.
